About me

Life History

My name is Tabitha Aluel from Southern Sudan. I have two brothers and one sister. I can say I have passed through many difficulties during childhood. I was born in Kakuma. The place was so hot that anyone would barely afford to wear clothes, especially at night. I then relocated to Eldoret where I am still living to date. My dream course has always been law. But due to unavoidable circumstances, I have not achieved it. Remember, God doesn't close one door without getting ready to open another with bigger and greater things. Actually, I was able to find another opportunity in Akirachix. Currently, I am studying Information Technology at AkiraChix. I believe by the end of this program, I would be somewhere working and earning.
